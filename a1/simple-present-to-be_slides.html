The forms

Positive, negative and question

Positive

I am a doctor.
She is ready.
They are late.

Negative

I am not tired.
He isn't here.
We aren't late.

Question

Am I late?
Is she your teacher?
Are you ready?

Wh- questions: Where are they from? · What is your name?

What can it describe?

To be describes almost everything.

Jobs & roles

She is a nurse. They are teachers.

Feelings & states

I am tired. It is cold today.

Origin & nationality

We are from Brazil. He is Italian.

In real sentences

See it in action

I am a student. My teacher is very kind.
We aren't late — we are five minutes early!
Is she your teacher? — Yes, she is.
Where are they from? — They are from Argentina.
⚠ Watch out

Common mistakes

They is happy.

They are happy.

He are my friend.

He is my friend.

She is a doctor? (as a question)

Is she a doctor?

I not am tired.

I am not tired.
