Simple Past:
Did

She went home. She didn't stay long.

past form did / didn't
The structure

How does it work?

Positive: subject + past form. Negative & question: use did / didn't — main verb always base form.

Examples

She finished the report before lunch.

I didn't go to school — I was ill.

Did you enjoy the concert?

The rule

Pos: subject + past form

Neg: didn't + base verb

Q: Did + subj + base verb?

The forms

Positive, negative and question

Positive

She finished the report.
We went to the cinema.

Negative

She didn't finish the report.
We didn't go to the cinema.

Question

Did she finish the report?
Did you go to the cinema?

Wh- questions: What did they have for dinner? · Where did you go?

She didn't went to the party.

I goed to the park after school.

I went to the park after school.
