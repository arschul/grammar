Reflexive
Pronouns

I cut myself. She did it herself.

myself / yourself himself / herself ourselves / themselves
The forms

One for each subject pronoun

I
myself
I cut myself while cooking.
you (sing.)
yourself
Did you enjoy yourself?
he / she / it
himself / herself / itself
He made himself a sandwich.
we / you (pl.) / they
ourselves / yourselves / themselves
We enjoyed ourselves.
Use 1 — same person

Subject and object refer to the same person

I see myself as a professional person.
She blamed herself for the mistake.
He hurt himself during training.
The door closed itself.
Use 2 — emphasis

Alone, without help

Did you build the fan club yourself, or did you have help? (at the end)
She decorated the apartment herself.
We cooked the entire meal ourselves.
⚠ Watch out

Common mistakes

He hurt hisself.

He hurt himself.

They did it theirself.

They did it themselves.

Yourself should try harder.

You should try harder. (reflexive ≠ subject)

We enjoyed ourself.

We enjoyed ourselves.
